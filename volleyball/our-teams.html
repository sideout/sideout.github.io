Our Teams

We have 7 teams based in both West and East London, delivering high quality volleyball for female and male players of all ages and abilities.

Find out more here and get in touch if you’re interested in joining us!


Team SideOut Polonia

Women’s team with a long and rich history of success at the highest level of volleyball in England, as well as internationally. The team competes in the Volleyball England Super League, the National Cup and international competitions and is made up of a diverse group of players, many with overseas professional volleyball experience. 2022 Women’s National Cup champions.

Westminster, West London.


Team SideOut XULU

Men’s team with over 20 years of history in the London League. Currently competing in Division 1 of the London League and in the National Cup.

Westminster, West London.


Team SideOut Women 1

Women’s team currently competing in the London Premier League and the National Cup. Runners-up of the 2022 London Premier League.

Westminster, West London.


Team SideOut Women 2

Women’s developmental team, training and competing in Division 2 of the London League.

Westminster, West London.


Team SideOut Academy Girls - West

Junior girls’ development programme, for girls of all abilities aged 8-18. We focus on providing our young athletes with top quality opportunities to train, play and compete while having fun and improving their fitness in a friendly and safe environment.

Westminster, West London.


Team SideOut Academy Boys - West

Junior boys’ development programme, for boys of all abilities aged 8-18. We focus on providing our young athletes with top quality opportunities to train, play and compete while having fun, improving their fitness and developing their skills in a friendly and safe environment.

Westminster, West London.


Team SideOut Academy Girls & Boys - East

Junior girls’ and boys’ development programme, for girls and boys of all abilities aged 5-13. Our goal is to combine fun with top quality volleyball coaching in a friendly and safe environment where our young athletes can make new friends, improve their fitness and develop their skills.

Leytonstone, East London.
