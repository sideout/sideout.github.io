Welcome to Team SideOut!

This exciting new volleyball project is the result of SideOut’s merger with Polonia Ladies Volleyball Club and ULU Volleyball Club, three clubs with a combined history of over 70 incredibly successful years.

With 8 teams training and competing at all levels from beginner to elite, local to international and junior to senior, we are sure to have plenty of opportunities for you to get involved!
