SideOut Summer Social in partnership with Weroad



The sand is our playground!

The SideOut Summer Social is all about the SideOut Family coming together and combining the laid-back vibes of the beach with the thrill of games and competition.

Featuring a day of play, drinks, meeting new friends and good times on the sands of Leyton Beach! With delicious food served up, great tunes blasting and lots of fun and games with awesome prizes to be won, this brand new event is not to be missed!

Open to all levels, from those who have never played before to seasoned pros and anything in between!
