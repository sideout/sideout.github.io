SideOut London Grand Prix



An Elite Series in London

The SideOut London Grand Prix is a series of three elite beach volleyball tournaments taking place at Leyton Beach throughout the summer.

Teams will earn SideOut Grand Prix Points at each event. At the end of the series, the teams with the most points will qualify for the SideOut London Finals in September. We will announce more details on the Finals soon, but we can assure you that it will be an amazing event you won't want to miss!

Key features:

  • 2-day events with full scoring in all matches.
  • Entry to each tournament determined by official National Ranking points.
  • £600 cash, prizes and medals at each event for the top 3 teams in each gender.
  • In September, the teams with the most SideOut Grand Prix Points will qualify for the SideOut London Finals! Full details coming soon, but we guarantee you won't want to miss this one!

TIMES
  • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm each day.
  • Detailed schedule and timings are provided by email no later than the day before each event.
FORMAT
  • 2-day events.
  • Pool play, followed by single elimination rounds.
  • Quarterfinals, semifinals and finals played on day 2.
  • Full international scoring (21-21-15) in all matches.
  • Minimum 3 matches per team.
  • Men's and women's draws run alongside each other at each tournament.
ENTRY RULES & PROCESS
  • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
  • All players must hold a valid SideOut Membership or Single Tournament Pass.
  • Registration closes on the Monday before each tournament.
  • If more than 24 entries are received, teams will be ranked by their National Ranking points and the top 24 ranked entrants will be eligible to participate. All other teams will be placed on the reserve list and have their entry fee refunded if they do not get into the tournament.
  • In case of a tie in National Ranking points, previous results in SideOut tournaments will also be taken into consideration.
  • Up to 2 wildcards per gender may be granted in exceptional circumstances and at SideOut's absolute discretion. If wildcards are granted, only the top 22/23 ranked teams (depending on number of wildcards) will be eligible.
  • All teams will receive an email by the Tuesday before each tournament informing them if they are in the tournament or not.
  • Detailed schedule and timings will be emailed to all teams no later than the day before each event.
  • These tournaments are not part of the UK Beach Tour and do not qualify for UKBT ranking points.
ENTRY FEES
  • £46 per team per tournament

CANCELLATION POLICY
  • You can cancel your tournament entry up to 7 days before the first day of the tournament and receive a full refund. Please email us using the contact details below to request cancellation.
  • If you cancel your tournament entry within 7 days of the tournament, you will not receive a refund unless the tournament is full and your team is replaced directly by a team on our waiting list.
  • No refunds will be given for cancellations requested within 48 hours of the start of the tournament.
