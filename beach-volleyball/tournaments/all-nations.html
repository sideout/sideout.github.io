All Nations Beach Volleyball



Represent your country!

VolleyFirst and SideOut are excited to announce that the All Nations Beach Volleyball tournament is returning to Leyton Beach with a bang this year!

NEXT EVENTS
  • Sat 8 & Sun 9 July 2023: Men's & Women's 2 vs 2.
    • Semifinals and finals to be played on Sunday afternoon.
  • Sunday 9 July 2023: Mixed 2 vs 2.
LOCATION
  • Leyton Beach, London
FORMAT & RULES
  • Men's and Women's 2 vs 2:
    • 2-day event.
    • Pool play and all matches up to quarterfinals on Saturday 8 July, with semifinals and finals on Sunday 9 July.
    • Semifinals and finals to be played on Sunday afternoon.
    • Entry is limited to 24 men's and 24 women's teams.
  • Mixed 2 vs 2:
    • 1-day event.
    • All matches on Sunday 9 July.
    • Entry is limited to 32 teams.
  • All tournaments:
    • You must enter as a team.
    • Both players on a team must be from the same country. Playing with someone from a different country will only be allowed in exceptional circumstances and at the organisers' discretion!
    • Minimum 4 matches per team per tournament.
    • Times: Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm each day.
    • Detailed schedule and timings will be provided by email no later than the Friday before the event.
PLAYING TOPS
  • All players will get an awesome customised All Nations beach volleyball playing top included with their entry!
  • Men get regular vests, women get female-cut vests (not crop-tops or sports bras).
  • Once you have entered online, please respond to the confirmation email with the following information:
    • Your team's country;
    • Your and your partner's clothing size (from S to XXL);
    • Your and your partner's kit number (one player must wear #1 and the other #2);
    • Optional: Your name to be printed on the kit (this is optional, at an extra cost of £10 per team, or £5 per player. The extra cost can be paid on the day of the tournament).
HOW TO ENTER
  • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
  • All players must hold a valid SideOut Membership or Single Tournament Pass.
  • To guarantee a personalised playing top you must enter by Monday 5th June
  • Entry is first-come, first-served. Once you have entered and paid, you are in the tournament!
  • All teams will receive an email with full details, schedule and timings by the day before each event.
HOW MUCH IS IT?
  • Men's and Women's 2 vs 2:
    • £55 per team.
  • Mixed 2 vs 2:
    • £55 per team.
Team numbers are strictly limited, so get your entry in early to avoid disappointment!

CANCELLATION POLICY
  • You can cancel your tournament entry up to 7 days before the first day of the tournament and receive a full refund. Please email us using the contact details below to request cancellation.
  • If you cancel your tournament entry within 7 days of the tournament, you will not receive a refund unless the tournament is full and your team is replaced directly by a team on our waiting list.
  • No refunds will be given for cancellations requested within 48 hours of the start of the tournament.
