SideOut London Grand Prix & Challenger Cup



An Exciting New London Series!

The SideOut London Grand Prix and the SideOut London Challenger Cup feature three action-packed weekends of beach volleyball taking place at Leyton Beach across the summer, culminating in a huge event in September: the SideOut London Finals at London's Canary Wharf!

SIDEOUT LONDON GRAND PRIX

  • 2-day elite-level events with full international scoring in all matches.

  • £600 cash, prizes and medals at each event for the top 3 teams in each gender.

  • Teams will earn London Grand Prix Points at each event. At the end of the series, the teams with the most points will qualify for the SideOut London Grand Prix Finals at London's Canary Wharf in September. We will announce more details on the Finals soon, but we guarantee it will be an amazing event and a unique experience you won't want to miss!

  • Format:
    • Pool play, followed by single elimination rounds.
    • Quarterfinals, semifinals and finals played on day 2.
    • Full international scoring (21-21-15) in all matches.
    • Minimum 3 matches per team.
    • Men's and women's draws run alongside each other at each tournament.
  • Times:
    • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm each day.
    • Detailed schedule and timings are provided by email no later than the day before each event.
  • Entry Rules & Process:
    • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
    • All players must hold a valid SideOut Membership or Single Tournament Pass.
    • Registration closes on the Monday before each tournament.
    • If more than 24 entries are received, teams will be ranked by their National Ranking points and the top 24 ranked entrants will be eligible to participate. All other teams will be placed on the reserve list and have their entry fee refunded if they do not get into the tournament.
    • In case of a tie in National Ranking points, previous results in SideOut tournaments will also be taken into consideration.
    • Up to 2 wildcards per gender may be granted in exceptional circumstances and at SideOut's absolute discretion. If wildcards are granted, only the top 22/23 ranked teams (depending on number of wildcards) will be eligible.
    • All teams will receive an email by the Tuesday before each tournament informing them if they are in the tournament or not.
    • Detailed schedule and timings will be emailed to all teams no later than the day before each event.
    • These tournaments are not part of the UK Beach Tour, do not require a UKBT membership to participate in, and do not qualify for UKBT ranking points.
  • Entry fees: £46 per team per tournament

SIDEOUT LONDON CHALLENGER CUP

  • 1-day tournaments aimed specifically at intermediate level players.

  • Cash, prizes and medals at each event for the top teams in each gender.

  • Teams will earn London Challenger Cup Points at each event. At the end of the series, the teams with the most points will qualify for the SideOut London Challenger Cup Finals at London's Canary Wharf in September. Full details coming soon, but we can assure you that it'll be an awesome event and a unique experience not to miss!

  • Format:
    • Men's and women's draws run alongside each other at each tournament
    • Pool play followed by elimination rounds
    • Minimum 3 matches per team per tournament
  • Times:
    • Approx. 9:00am-7:00pm
    • Detailed schedule and timings are provided by email no later than the day before each tournament.
  • Entry Rules & Process:
    • Register and pay online via registration.sideout.co.uk.
    • All players must hold a valid SideOut Membership or Single Tournament Pass.
    • Registration closes when the tournament is full, or on the Friday before each tournament.
    • Entry is first-come, first-served. Once you have entered and paid, you are in the tournament!
    • All teams will receive an email with full details, schedule and timings by the day before each tournament.
    • These tournaments are not part of the UK Beach Tour, do not require a UKBT membership to participate in, and do not qualify for UKBT ranking points.
  • Entry fees: £38 per team per tournament

CANCELLATION POLICY
  • You can cancel your tournament entry up to 7 days before the first day of the tournament and receive a full refund. Please email us using the contact details below to request cancellation.
  • If you cancel your tournament entry within 7 days of the tournament, you will not receive a refund unless the tournament is full and your team is replaced directly by a team on our waiting list.
  • No refunds will be given for cancellations requested within 48 hours of the start of the tournament.
