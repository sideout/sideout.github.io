Spain Training Camp


Playa de San Juan, 22nd-28th May 2023

This spring, SideOut's famous beach volleyball training camps are back! We'll be heading to the beautiful sands of Playa de San Juan, near Alicante in Spain, from the 22nd to the 28th of May 2023!

Suitable for all ages and abilities, this camp is not only a perfect opportunity to get top quality and high intensity beach volleyball training, but it will also be a truly memorable week with amazing people on one of Spain's most beautiful beaches and one of Europe's greatest beach volleyball hotspots.

As if that all wasn't enough, the coaching staff includes an Olympic medallist and some incredibly experienced players and coaches, who will all be on hand throughout the camp to help take your game to the next level.

Full details are below. To ensure quality and the best possible experience for you, spaces are strictly limited - so be sure to book early and avoid disappointment! We look forward to welcoming you to Playa de San Juan!

LOCATION

  • Playa de San Juan is situated just 20 minutes from Alicante airport and 10 minutes from the centre of Alicante by car - or slightly longer by bus or tramway.

  • The courts are situated near the famous multi-coloured wooden cabins on the seafront. They have medium-depth sand, which is ideal for training in the early part of the season.

  • There are plenty of nearby restaurants, bars and clubs and plenty of sights to see such as the lighthouse landmark and local parks. There is also a beach gym and lots of sports facilities available for use.

  • Alicante is renowned for the famous St Barbara Castle, situated on the hills of the town. You can also visit the famous “Mushroom street” and the local harbour, not to mention trying out the amazing flavours of the delicious local cuisine.
