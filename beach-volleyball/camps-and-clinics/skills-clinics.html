SideOut Skills Clinics

The premier beach volleyball clinics for players of all ages and abilities.


Take your beach volleyball game to the next level!

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced player, our clinics are designed to improve your skills, knowledge, and overall performance on the sand. Led by our team of expert coaches, each clinic will provide you with comprehensive and tailored training sessions, game play sessions, and opportunities to practice your skills.

Our clinics are held at various venues throughout the 2023 beach volleyball season, including Leyton Beach and Worthing Beach, with more to be announced soon. Each clinic includes 2 x 90-minute training sessions and 1 x 90-minute game play session, with breaks in between to help you recover and recharge.

We pride ourselves on the quality of our coaching and our training sessions. We keep our training groups small, with never more than 6 players training on 1 court. This ensures that each participant receives personalised attention and feedback from our coaches, making our clinics both effective and enjoyable.

Our clinics are focused on specific areas of the game, such as passing and setting, service and reception, block and defence, setting and attacking, and more. You can sign up as an individual or in a group of 6, and the price is only £35 per person for each clinic.

Don't miss our first clinic at Leyton Beach on Saturday 15th April 2023! More dates and locations will be announced soon, so stay tuned for updates.
