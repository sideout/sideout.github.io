FIVB Beach Volleyball Coaching Course - Athens



Athens, Greece, 11th-15th October 2023

Following the tremendous success of the courses we co-hosted with the Hellenic Volleyball Federation over the last 4 years, we are returning to the Greek capital next October to deliver one more FIVB Beach Volleyball Level 1 Coaching Course.

Full details are below. To ensure quality and the best possible experience for all participants, spaces are strictly limited - so be sure to book early and avoid disappointment! We look forward to welcoming you to Athens!

Registration Opens Soon!

COURSE INFO

  • Structure & requirements:

    - Part 1: candidates complete a series of e-Learning modules. These can be accessed on demand and should be completed before the start of Part 2.

    - Part 2: candidates attend the practical course in Athens over 5 days (40 hours total).

    - Part 3: candidates complete a practical assessment on day 5 of the practical course.

  • Course times: 08:30-17:30 each day from Wednesday 11th October to Sunday 15th October 2023

  • Application deadline: Sunday 24th September 2023

  • Instructors:

    - Course Director: Jeff Brehaut (ENG)

    - Assistant Instructor: Mark Kontopoulos (ENG/GRE)

    - Assistant Instructor: Alex Chevau (FRA)

    - More guest instructors to be confirmed!

  • Links:

    - www.fivb.org

    - www.volleyball.gr


LOGISTICS

  • Venue: To be confirmed soon.

  • Departures:

REGISTRATION & FEES

  • Applications must be made online via registration.sideout.co.uk.

  • Deadline to apply: Sunday 24th September 2023

  • Application and Entry Process:

    - Once you submit your online application, you will receive an email with instructions for next steps and payment. The organisers may request a letter of recommendation from your National Federation before your application can be accepted.

    - Participation on the course is 'first come, first served' and spaces are strictly limited. Your participation will not be confirmed until we have received your application, letter of recommendation (if required) and payment.

  • Course fees:

    - Greek participants: 350 Euros per person (cost subsidised by Hellenic Volleyball Federation for its members)

    - International participants: 440 Euros per person.

    - Prices include water, snacks and coffee.

    - They do NOT include accommodation, transfers, breakfast, lunch or dinner.

  • Payments:

    - All payments must be made by bank transfer in Euros.

    - We will provide bank details by email following receipt of your application.

CANCELLATION POLICY
  • You can cancel your participation up to 21 days before the first day of the course and receive a full refund. Please email us using the contact details below to request cancellation.
  • If you cancel your participation within 21 days of the course, you will not receive a refund unless the course is full and you are replaced directly by a candidate on our waiting list.
  • No refunds will be given for cancellations requested within 10 days of the start of the course.
