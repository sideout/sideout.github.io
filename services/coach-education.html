Coach Education

Working in partnership with sports federations, clubs and other stakeholders, we are leaders in volleyball and beach volleyball coach education in the UK and Europe.


Transforming coaching and empowering coaches to excel

We offer accredited coaching courses and specialist clinics in both volleyball and beach volleyball, covering both the theory and practice of coaching. Our experienced and internationally recognised team of educators and instructors brings a unique philosophy, which is designed to empower coaches by providing them with the knowledge and tools to:

  • effectively teach all technical, tactical and mental aspects of the game;
  • enhance their own practical skills;
  • inspire and connect more deeply with their athletes;
  • enable their athletes to unlock their full potential; and
  • achieve success both on and off the court.

Our mission is to provide coaches of all levels with the opportunity to learn and grow, so all our courses and programmes are tailored to meet the specific needs of participants. And for maximum flexibility and accessibility, most of our sessions can be made available both online and face-to-face.

If you would like us to organise and deliver a coaching course for your organisation, we would love to hear from you. Just get in touch for an informal discussion and we will be more than happy to help.

If you are a coach or aspiring coach interested in joining a course or clinic, scroll down to see what we currently have on offer. We look forward to seeing you on the court and in the classroom and helping you take your coaching to the next level!
